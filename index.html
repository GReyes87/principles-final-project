    About Our Project

    Our websites main purpose is to offer valuable insight on the Leading Causes of Death in the US. Through clear and descriptive visualizations, this website sheds light on trends, and helps contribute to the efforts of public health workers. The Data oversees certain Leading Causes of Deaths in the US from 1999 - 2017.
